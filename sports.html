The approach

Sport is drama.
I just find the frame.

A boxing ring entrance with pyrotechnics. A striker locking eyes with the goalkeeper. A fighter in the locker room before the bell. These are cinematic moments — and most sports photographers miss them.

I shoot sport the way a film director would: looking for tension, character, and atmosphere. The result feels less like a press photo and more like a movie still.
